About Us

Totaram Weldmesh Industries

We "Totaram Weldmesh Industries" were incorporated in the year 2014, as a leading manufacturer and trader of a broad range of Welded Mesh, Binding Wire, Barbed Wire and Chain Link Fencing for our customers.. The major strength of our company is our reputed vendors, employees, and customers enabling us to reach the peak of success. Constant innovation along with high quality standards assists us to meet the market needs and demands with value added products. Our wide product range is appreciated all around for its quality and availability in different shapes and sizes. We are successfully catering to the varied demands of our esteem clients from various sectors including real estate projects, construction, security, education, mining, hospitality and facilities management for diverse industries. Today, Our company attained a position in the market is the result of our hard work and dedication. We are continuously progressing under the able guidance of our mentor, Mr. Anubhav Gupta. Capitalizing on his experience and expertise in this industry, we have been able to carve a niche for ourselves in this domain. Our effective marketing strategy and client centric approach also assists us in catering to the needs of a wide clientele.
